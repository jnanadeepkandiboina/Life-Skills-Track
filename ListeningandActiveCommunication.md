**1. Active Listening** (Question 1)
1. The steps/strategies to do Active Listening are:
 - Pay full attention to the speaker.
 - Avoid Interupting when others speaking.
 - Show you are listening by body language such as nodding, etc.
 - Make them to continue speaking by saying "That's Intersting.." or "What's next.." or "go on.."
 - Take notes if necessary.
 - Reflect back what you listened that make sure you are in same page as them.

**2. Reflective Listening** 
2. According to Fisher's model, what are the key points of Reflective Listening?
Ans: Reflective Listening means really paying attention to what other person is saying and how they feel. You repeat their to show you understood them correctly.
    You also pick up on their emotions and body language. This helps both people stay on the same page as them and avoid distracting and confusion.

**3. Reflection**
3. What are the obstacles in your listening process?
Ans:
 - Getting distracted by other things(like mobile, etc).
 - Daydreaming.
 - Interupting the speaker.
 - Being emotionally biased.

4. What can you do to improve your listening?
Ans:
  - Paying full attention on speaker without distraction.
  - Avoid interupting.
  - Summerizing what you hear.
  - By asking questions if something is unclear.
  - Reflecting back for what you listened.
