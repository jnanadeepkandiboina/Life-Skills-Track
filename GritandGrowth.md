**3. What is the Internal Locus of Control? What is the key point in the video?**
---
**Ans:** An internal locus of control means believing that your actions and effort directly influence the outcome in your life. The main point in the video is that when you see yourself as responsible for your success, you stay more motivated take on challenges, and keep pushing through difficulities.
