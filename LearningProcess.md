Ans: Active mode of thinking is when your brain is fully focused on a task. Diffused mode of thinking means when your mind is relaxed.

**4. According to the video, what are the steps to take when approaching a new topic? Only mention the points.**

Ans: 
  - Break the main skill into smaller, manageable sub-skills.
  - Identify the most important sub-skills to start with.
  - Learn just enough from reliable resources to practice and self-correct.
  - Remove all distractions while practicing.
  - Commit at least 20 hours to initial learning and practice.
